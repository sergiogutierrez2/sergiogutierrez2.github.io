Memory Game help

            

The game has 6 pairs
of similar images:
2 Lions
2 Tigers
2 Jaguars
2 Panthers
2 Cheetahs
2 Leopards

Flip two of the same two images
to maintain your score of 10.
Flip two different, and your score
will go down. If your score
goes to 0, you lose.

For every wrong flip in the
game, you lose 1 point.

Good Luck!!

You restart a game by clicking
on the Restart Game button.
