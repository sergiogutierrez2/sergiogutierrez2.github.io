MasterMind Help

            

Instructions:
The game has chosen 4 colors,
and has placed them
in certain positions (from left to right),
in this manner: ?, ?, ?, ?
Guess the colors and their positions to win.

The first step is to click on
any of the 6 possible colors:
  B = Blue
  W = White
  G = Green
  P = Purple
  R = Red
  Y = Yellow

Then click on the squares with question
marks to populate them with the
color(s) you choose.

You may choose to populate
them with different
colors, or the same color
any number of times.

Then click on "Check Answer."

You ask the game to
check your four colors and
and their positions by
clicking the Check Answer button.

The game displays your guess
and provides you clues.

The clues mean:
  X = one right color, right position.
  O = one right color, bad position.

Any 'X' shown could be any color.
IE, if Guess shows B W G Y and
clues are 'XX', could be either:
B and W, B and G, B and Y,
W and G, W and Y, OR G and Y
are the right colors in the right
position.

You have 10 chances to win.

You restart a game by clicking
on the Restart Game button.
